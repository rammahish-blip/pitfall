how to play

each pick is one turn
Tap any amber vault. All its gems drop one-by-one clockwise around the loop. That counts as your full turn. You have a limited number of picks — plan ahead to hit your target.
collect across the gap
After your last gem drops — if the next vault is empty and the vault after has gems, you collect them all! If the next vault has gems, the turn ends with no collect. Plan your drops carefully.
mystery vaults
Some levels have mystery vaults — you can't see how many gems are inside. Tap them anyway — the count reveals the moment you pick, then gems drop as normal. Risk it or play safe — your call.
