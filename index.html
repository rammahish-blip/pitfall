how to play

each pick is one turn
Tap any amber vault. All its gems drop one-by-one clockwise around the loop. That's your full turn. You have a limited number of picks — choose carefully to hit your target.
collect across the gap
After your last gem drops — if the next vault is empty and the vault after it has gems, you collect them all! If the next vault has gems, the turn ends with no collect. Plan your picks to land in the right spot.
mystery vaults
Later levels have mystery vaults — you don't know how many gems are inside. Tap them anyway — the count reveals the moment you pick, then gems drop as normal. Risk or safety — your call.
