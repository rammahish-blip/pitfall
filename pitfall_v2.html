how to play
skip
pick a vault
Tap any amber vault on the board. You pick up all the gems inside it — that vault becomes empty and the gems are in your hand, ready to drop.
gems drop around the loop
One gem drops into each vault going clockwise. When you reach the end of a row the gems wrap around to the other row and keep going.
collect across the gap
After your last drop — if the next vault is empty, check the one after it. Gems there? You collect them all! If the next vault has gems, it turns pink — tap it to keep going.
